About Me

Hello! I'm Michael, a passionate web developer, and graphic designer based in Nigeria.
With a strong background in front-end development and UI/UX design, I thrive on turning complex ideas into simple, beautiful, and user-friendly designs.
Over the past months, I’ve worked on diverse projects, ranging from a responsive Real estate portfolio to a responsive calculator.
My approach is always centred on innovation, & collaboration, ensuring that every project I work on meets and exceeds expectations.
I graduated from the University of Michigan with a Computer science and graphic design degree, which provided me with a solid foundation in problem-solving and creative thinking.
Since then, I’ve continually expanded my skill set, learning new tools and techniques to stay ahead in the fast-evolving tech/design world.
When I’m not busy coding or designing, you can find me exploring the outdoors, reading up on the latest tech trends, or experimenting with new design techniques.
I’m always open to new challenges and opportunities, so if you have a project in mind or just want to say hello, feel free to connect with me. Thank you for visiting my portfolio.
I look forward to collaborating with you on your next project!.
